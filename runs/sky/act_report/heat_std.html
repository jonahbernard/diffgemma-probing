heatmap: global std

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
Standard deviation over every element of the activation tensor -- the typical spread of values. Compare against 'global |activation| max': if absmax is huge but std is small, a few outliers dominate an otherwise small tensor (the bad case for fp4).

x = denoising step, y = decoder layer. diff = mxfp4 - bf16 (red = mxfp4 larger).
