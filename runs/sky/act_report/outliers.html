outlier rankings

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
Leaderboards of the most extreme single activations in the whole run. Each pair of tables ranks the top-20 (layer, GEMM site, denoising step) combinations for one metric -- bf16 on the left, mxfp4 on the right -- so you can jump straight to where outliers are worst instead of hunting through heatmaps. value is that metric's number; site/layer/step tell you exactly which activation to inspect on the per-channel or per-token pages. Metrics: worst-channel absmax (outlier channels), worst-token absmax & kurtosis (outlier tokens), max per-block absmax and its inflation ratio (fp4 cost), and outlier mass (frac |a|>k*std).
