heatmap: max per-block absmax (fp4 worst block)

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
fp4 quantizes in contiguous blocks of 32 channels, giving each block one shared scale set by that block's largest magnitude (its absmax). This is the LARGEST such block-absmax in the tensor -- the worst-quantized block, where one outlier forces a coarse scale on its 31 neighbors.

x = denoising step, y = decoder layer. diff = mxfp4 - bf16 (red = mxfp4 larger).
