heatmap: worst-token kurtosis

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
Kurtosis measures peakedness: a Gaussian is 3, higher means a few channels dominate that token's vector. This is the highest per-token kurtosis in the tensor. High = some token's energy is concentrated in a handful of channels (spiky), which is exactly what fp4 blocks handle poorly.

x = denoising step, y = decoder layer. diff = mxfp4 - bf16 (red = mxfp4 larger).
