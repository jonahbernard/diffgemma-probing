heatmap: fraction |a| > k*std (outlier mass)

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
Fraction of elements whose magnitude exceeds k*std (k=6 by default) -- i.e. what share of the tensor counts as outliers. 0.001 = 0.1% of values are extreme. Small but non-zero is the classic outlier signature: rare huge values riding on top of a tight distribution.

x = denoising step, y = decoder layer. diff = mxfp4 - bf16 (red = mxfp4 larger).
