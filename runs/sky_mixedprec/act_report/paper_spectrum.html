paper: channel spectrum

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
AWQ / LLM.int8() / QuaRot analog. For one GEMM site we take each channel's magnitude (per-channel absmax) and reduce over all layers, then: Left = that magnitude sorted high-to-low on a log y-axis -- the canonical 'salient channel' spectrum showing how a tiny fraction of channels tower 10-100x over the rest (AWQ picks exactly these; LLM.int8() routes them to higher precision). Right = the histogram of per-channel magnitudes (log y) -- the heavy-tail view that QuaRot/SpinQuant show shrinking after rotation. green = bf16, red = mxfp4; a longer/heavier red tail means mxfp4 is carrying larger activation extremes into fp4. One row per GEMM site.
