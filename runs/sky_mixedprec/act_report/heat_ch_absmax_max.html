heatmap: worst-channel absmax

magenta/red = mxfp4 (W4A4), green = bf16. Outliers inflate fp4 block scales, so big per-block absmax = more quantization error.
What am I looking at?
Each channel (hidden dim) has a max-magnitude over all tokens; this is the largest of those over all channels -- the single most extreme channel. High = an 'outlier channel' (one hidden dim that is persistently large). See the per-channel pages to find WHICH channel index.

x = denoising step, y = decoder layer. diff = mxfp4 - bf16 (red = mxfp4 larger).
